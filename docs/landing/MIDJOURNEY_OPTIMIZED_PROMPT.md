## 📋 PARAMETERS EXPLAINED:

### **--style raw**
- **Effect**: Less stylized, more realistic look
- **Why**: Creates more natural, authentic health imagery
- **Result**: More realistic colors and textures

### **--chaos 30**
- **Effect**: Introduces natural variation in color blending
- **Why**: Creates organic, natural color transitions
- **Result**: More varied and interesting color mixing

### **--quality 2**
- **Effect**: Higher rendering fidelity, smoother composition
- **Why**: Better detail and smoother visuals
- **Result**: Premium, high-quality output

### **--ar 9:16**
- **Effect**: Portrait aspect ratio (mobile format)
- **Why**: Perfect for landing page background
- **Result**: Correct dimensions for mobile

### **--v 6**
- **Effect**: Latest Midjourney version
- **Why**: Best quality and features
- **Result**: Most advanced generation
