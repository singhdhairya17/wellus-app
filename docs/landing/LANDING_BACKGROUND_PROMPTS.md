# Landing Background Prompts - Peaceful, Calm, Healthy

## 🎨 MAIN PROMPT (Copy & Paste This):

```
Create a peaceful, calm, and healthy background image for a wellness mobile app landing page.

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:
- Full-screen mobile background (portrait orientation, 9:16 aspect ratio)
- Peaceful, serene, and calming atmosphere
- Health and wellness theme
- Meaningful symbolism related to health, balance, and vitality
- Soft, gentle colors that complement teal (#14B8A6) and green (#10B981)
- Premium, modern aesthetic
- Subtle and not distracting (will have dark overlay and text on top)

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Abstract or subtle nature elements (leaves, water, light, organic shapes)
- Soft gradients or gentle patterns
- Peaceful, zen-like atmosphere
- Health and wellness symbolism (subtle, not literal)
- Calming, therapeutic feeling
- Natural, organic flow

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Soft teal (#14B8A6) and green (#10B981) tones
- Complementary: Soft blues, gentle greens, warm whites
- Mood: Calming, peaceful, fresh, natural
- Avoid: Harsh colors, high contrast, busy patterns

STYLE:
- Minimalist and clean
- Soft focus or subtle blur effect
- Gentle gradients
- Organic, flowing shapes
- Premium, sophisticated
- Modern, contemporary
- Peaceful, zen aesthetic

MOOD & FEELING:
- Peaceful and serene
- Calming and therapeutic
- Healthy and vibrant
- Balanced and harmonious
- Fresh and natural
- Wellness-focused
- Meaningful and inspiring

TECHNICAL:
- Portrait orientation (9:16 ratio)
- High resolution (1440x2560px or higher)
- Soft, subtle details (not sharp or harsh)
- Works with dark overlay on top
- Premium quality

INSPIRATION:
Think spa, wellness retreat, meditation, nature therapy, health sanctuary - 
peaceful, calm, meaningful, and healthy atmosphere.
```
