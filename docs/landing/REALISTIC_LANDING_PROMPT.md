- Color grading: Professional, cinematic, premium

COMPOSITION (Professional Photography):

- Main subjects in focus
- Natural flow and movement
- Balanced, harmonious layout

SPECIFIC ELEMENTS (Realistic Details):
- Fresh, vibrant fruits and vegetables (photorealistic)
- Lush green leaves and plants (realistic texture)
- Natural water droplets, freshness

- Real food photography aesthetic
- Natural lighting and shadows
- Professional depth of field
- Realistic textures and surfaces

MOOD & ATMOSPHERE:
- Fresh and invigorating
- Healthy and vibrant
- Premium and sophisticated
- Natural and authentic
- Calming yet energetic
- Professional and trustworthy
- Inspiring and motivating
- Meaningful health symbolism

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Portrait orientation (9:16 aspect ratio)
- High resolution (1440x2560px or higher)
- Photorealistic quality
- Professional color grading
- Works with dark overlay on top
- Premium, cinematic quality

AVOID:
- Cartoon style
- Illustration style
- Abstract art
- Stylized graphics
- Flat design
- Overly saturated colors
- Unrealistic elements

INSPIRATION:
Think professional health food photography, premium wellness brand imagery, 
cinematic nature photography, realistic food styling - photorealistic, 
sophisticated, meaningful, matches Wellus health and wellness app motives.
