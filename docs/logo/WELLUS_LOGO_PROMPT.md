# Premium Logo Design Prompt for Wellus

## 🎨 Perfect Prompt for LOGO GPT

### **MAIN PROMPT (Copy & Paste This):**

```
Create a premium, modern, minimalist logo for "Wellus" - a sophisticated health and wellness mobile application.

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:
- Square format (1:1 ratio) optimized for mobile app icon
- Ultra-minimalist and clean design - maximum simplicity
- Premium, luxury aesthetic with sophisticated elegance
- Unique and distinctive - stands out from competitors
- Modern contemporary style - 2024 design trends
- Single icon or symbol (no text) - icon-only design
- Geometric or abstract wellness symbol (leaf, heart, wave, circle, or unique abstract shape)
- Solid colors only - no gradients, no shadows, no effects
- High contrast for visibility on both light and dark backgrounds
- Scalable design that works perfectly at 100x100 pixels

COLOR SCHEME (Match App Theme):
- Primary color: Purple (#8B5CF6) or user's selected theme color
- Alternative colors: Blue (#3B82F6), Green (#10B981), Teal (#14B8A6), or Indigo (#6366F1)
- Single color or two-tone maximum
- Must work on white, dark gray (#1F1F1F), and light gray (#F5F5F5) backgrounds

STYLE GUIDELINES:
- Clean lines and simple shapes
- No decorative elements
- No text or typography
- No complex details
- Bold and recognizable at small sizes
- Professional and trustworthy appearance
- Wellness/health symbolism (subtle, not literal)
- Modern tech aesthetic

TECHNICAL SPECS:
- Transparent background (PNG format)
- High resolution (512x512 pixels minimum)
- Vector-style appearance (clean, crisp edges)
- No anti-aliasing artifacts
- Works in both light and dark themes

INSPIRATION:
Think Apple, Google, or premium health apps - clean, simple, memorable, and premium.
```
