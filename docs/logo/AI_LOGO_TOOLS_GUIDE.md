## 🚀 Quick Start Guide: Using Looka (Recommended)

### Step 1: Create Your Logo
1. Go to https://looka.com
2. Click "Get Started" or "Create Logo"
3. Enter brand name: **"Wellus"**
4. Select industry: **"Health & Wellness"** or **"Fitness"**
5. Choose style preferences:
   - Modern
   - Minimalist
   - Professional
6. Select color scheme:
   - Use your app's primary color (check ThemeContext)
   - Or choose: Blue/Green (health theme)

### Step 2: Customize
1. AI will generate multiple logo options
2. Browse and select your favorite
3. Customize:
   - Colors
   - Fonts
   - Icons
   - Layout

### Step 3: Download
1. Choose "Download Logo"
2. Select format: **PNG**
3. Size: **512x512** or higher
4. Enable: **Transparent Background**
5. Download the file

### Step 4: Replace in App
1. Save as `logo.png`
2. Replace file at: `assets/images/logo.png`
3. Test in app!

## 💡 Pro Tips for AI Logo Generation

### Input Suggestions for Wellus:
- **Brand Name**: Wellus
- **Industry**: Health & Wellness, Fitness, Nutrition
- **Style Keywords**: Modern, Clean, Minimalist, Professional
- **Color Suggestions**: 
  - Primary: Your app's PRIMARY color (check ThemeContext)
  - Secondary: Green (health), Blue (trust), or your SECONDARY color
- **Icon Ideas**: 
  - Leaf (wellness)
  - Heart (health)
  - Wave (flow, wellness)
  - Circle (wholeness)
  - Abstract geometric shapes

### What to Look For:
✅ Simple and clean design
✅ Works at small sizes (100x100px)
✅ Readable text if including "Wellus"
✅ High contrast for visibility
✅ Scalable (vector or high-res PNG)

### What to Avoid:
❌ Too complex designs
❌ Too many colors
❌ Thin lines that disappear at small sizes
❌ Text that's hard to read
❌ Cluttered compositions

## 🎯 Recommended Workflow

1. **Try Looka first** (best balance of quality and price)
2. **Generate 3-5 options** with different styles
3. **Test in your app** by replacing logo.png temporarily
4. **Refine** if needed using Canva or Figma
